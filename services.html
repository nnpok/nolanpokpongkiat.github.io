COMPREHENSIVE EYE EXAM
At Dr Si Optometry, Dr Si and our entire Los Angeles optometry team are committed to providing advanced vision care in a professional and comfortable environment. As an optometrist in Hollywood, our primary eye care service includes a complete eye exam that analyzes eye health and vision function. In addition to eye exams and vision testing, Dr. Si and our team of optometry professionals provide testing for glaucoma, cataracts and macular degeneration. We also offer glasses, contact lenses, and pre- and post-operative care. Eye exams should be scheduled yearly to monitor eye health and changes in prescription.

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT?
Throughout his 30 years of practice, Dr Si has seen a trend of patients that have come in with symptoms of headaches, migraines, dry eyes, dizziness, nausea, and light sensitivity. These patients have often been to multiple doctors from many specialties, but have been turned back and been told their symptoms are normal and not treatable. However, with research both in the lab and clinically derived from the work of Helen Irlen, Dr Si has developed a solution to these symptoms through the use of colored lenses. In short, this new treatment blocks harmful wavelengths of light from entering the eye, improving brain function.
